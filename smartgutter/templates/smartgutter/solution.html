Solution: SmartGutter AI System

A collaborative system between the city and homeowners to proactively avoid flood situations

SmartGutter AI is designed to create collaboration between the city and homeowners. The system collects data from sensors in storm gutters and uses notifications and alerts to provide details to both homeowners and city workers—enabling them to work together to ensure city drains are not clogged with debris.

Design & Technology

Three main components power the system

📡

Gutter Sensors

Sensors placed inside storm gutters collect data on water level, debris buildup, and other critical metrics in real time.

🧠

Predictive AI

Uses current and historical weather data combined with sensor data to predict the severity and size of potential flooding. This model is crucial for scheduling city resources.

📢

Notification System

Automated alerts are sent to the city and registered homeowners based on weather predictions and sensor readings.

Operations & Scheduling

City Resource Management

The AI model schedules gutter cleaning based on:

  • Predicted weather severity
  • Available city workers
  • Gutter priority and locations

Homeowner Mobilization

When city resources may be overwhelmed:

  • System predicts if additional help is required
  • Targeted alerts sent to registered homeowners
  • Homeowners assist with priority gutters near their location
