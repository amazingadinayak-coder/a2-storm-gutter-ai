User Experience & Collaboration

How city workers and homeowners participate in the SmartGutter AI service

For the City

1

Register Workers

The city registers workers who can perform gutter cleaning. These workers receive assignments and alerts based on AI predictions.

2

Provide Gutter Data

The city provides the number and locations of gutters that need cleaning, along with sensor deployment information.

3

Assign Priority

Cleaning priority is assigned based on the predicted size and severity of the storm. The AI helps optimize resource allocation.

For Homeowners

1

Register for Service

Homeowners register for the service and provide their location and contact information. This allows the system to match them with nearby gutters.

2

Get Matched

The system identifies ideal gutters near their location for them to assist with when their help is needed.

3

Receive Alerts

When help is needed, homeowners receive alerts and notifications to collaboratively unclog a specific gutter before a flood occurs.

Notifications and alerts are based on current and historical weather data. The scheduling of gutter cleaning uses weather data as well as available city resources—ensuring efficient, proactive flood prevention.
