Get Involved

Join SmartGutter AI—whether you're a city worker or a homeowner ready to help your community

🏛️ City Registration

Cities can register their workers and gutter infrastructure to participate in the SmartGutter AI network.

  • Register city workers who can clean gutters
  • Provide number of gutters and their locations
  • Set priority based on storm size and severity
  • Receive AI-driven scheduling recommendations

🏠 Homeowner Registration

Homeowners can sign up to receive alerts and help clean gutters near their location when storms approach.

  • Register with your location and contact info
  • Get matched with gutters near you
  • Receive alerts when your help is needed
  • Collaborate to prevent floods in your community

Coming Soon

Registration forms and full platform access are under development. This website presents the SmartGutter AI proposal and design. Contact your city officials to express interest in piloting this system.
